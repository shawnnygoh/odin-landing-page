Some common mental illnesses.

Persistent Depressive Disorder (PDD): Persistent Depressive Disorder is a chronic type of depression which can interfere with daily life.

General Anxiety Disorder (GAD): People with General Anxiety Disorder may find it hard to get through the day without being nervous about a number of things even when there is no apparent reason to be worried.

Major Depressive Disorder (MDD): Otherwise known as clinical depression, people with Major Depressive Disorder experience extreme sadness, hopelessness and may attempt to commit suicide on a regular basis.

Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD): This disorder causes constant repetitive thoughts about a certain thing, or obsessions, which can affect the daily lives of those who have it.
